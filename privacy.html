Privacy Policy

Your information is used solely to publish content to your connected social media accounts on your behalf.

Authorization tokens are stored locally on your device. We do not store your data on external servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties.

6. Your Rights

You can revoke access to your social media accounts at any time through the platform settings.
